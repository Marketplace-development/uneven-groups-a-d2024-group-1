Uploading your location is free for now. Our aim is to attract traffic and grow our user base. In the future, we plan to implement a degressive pricing model: the cost will depend on the number of available seats at your location. Larger venues with more seats will have a higher price tier when uploading their location.

Currently, you can upload one location at a time. If needed, you can delete your existing location and upload a new one.

To upload your location, we need the following:
- Location Name
- Type of Location (e.g., library, cafe, coffeehouse, tearoom, restaurant, or other)
- Number of Chairs Available
- Address
- Opening Hours

Unfortunately, updates are not allowed after a location is uploaded. If you need to make changes, you’ll need to delete your location and upload it again with the updated details.

If a booking is canceled or the guest doesn’t show up, you’ll be notified. We encourage you to reach out to the student for clarification.

Each week, we highlight the top-rated locations based on reviews from the previous week. This means offering great service and maintaining excellent reviews can help your location get featured and attract more visitors.

Making a reservation is free, but it is expected that you order something while at the location to support the business.

You can make as many reservations as you like, provided the locations have availability.

Absolutely! When making your reservation, simply select the number of people in your group, and the system will ensure enough seats are reserved for everyone.

There is no limit to how far in advance you can make a reservation, as long as the location has availability.

If you need to cancel, you can do so directly through your account. Simply navigate to the Your Reservations page and click Cancel Reservation. Please try to cancel as early as possible to free up the seat for others.

If you’re running late, we recommend notifying the location to ensure your seat isn’t released. Locations typically hold reservations for 15–30 minutes, but policies may vary.

Go to Your Reservations, and if you can see your reservation there, you can be sure that your reservation is confirmed! If you're still not certain, you can always contact the location owner.

Currently, you cannot modify an existing reservation. If changes are needed, please cancel the current reservation and make a new one with the updated details.
